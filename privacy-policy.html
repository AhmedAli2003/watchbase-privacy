1. Introduction

WatchBase is a mobile application that allows users to view movie and TV series information, including ratings and descriptions. Users can also save titles to their Favorites, Watch Later, or Watched lists.

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data on our own servers. However, we use Google Sign-In via Firebase Authentication to allow users to sign into the app. When you sign in, we may access the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture URL

This data is only used to complete the authentication process and personalize the user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your Google account information only for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity.
  • To personalize your app experience (e.g., display your name and profile picture).

We do not:

  • Store your personal data outside Firebase.
  • Sell or share your information with third parties.
  • Access or request any other data beyond what is provided by Google Sign-In.

4. Data Storage and Security

We rely on Firebase Authentication, a secure and trusted service by Google, to manage login and user identity. Your credentials are handled securely according to Google’s standards.
